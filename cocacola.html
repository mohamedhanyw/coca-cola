The History of Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola was invented in 1886 by pharmacist John Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia. It was originally marketed as a medicine and sold at soda fountains. In 1892, Asa Candler purchased the Coca-Cola formula and established the Coca-Cola Company. The iconic Coca-Cola logo was created in 1887 and has remained largely unchanged since then.

Coca-Cola Products

Coca-Cola offers a wide variety of products, including Coca-Cola Classic, Diet Coke, Coke Zero, Sprite, Fanta, and more. The company also offers various flavors and packaging options to cater to different preferences and occasions.

Coca-Cola Sustainability

Coca-Cola is committed to sustainability and has set a goal to become a "water-neutral" company. The company has also made strides in reducing its carbon footprint and promoting recycling and responsible sourcing.
